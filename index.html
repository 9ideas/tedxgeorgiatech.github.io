ABOUT US

What is TED?

TED is committed to starting a dialogue about ideas worth spreading. Each talk features fascinating ideas that demand to be heard. From scientific breakthroughs to inspiring human stories to unexpected psychological findings, TEDTalks has them all.

What about TEDxGeorgiaTech?

TEDxGeorgiaTech is an independently licensed TED event for Georgia Tech and the surrounding metro-Atlanta area. Our goal is to showcase the ideas from our own community and bring innovation and inspiration to make Tech into a community of change. At the event, a captivating litany of Atlanta locals will be sharing their personal experiences and stories about anything and everything, from street art to saving the world.
